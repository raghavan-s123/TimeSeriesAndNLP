Before performing advanced analysis or forecasting, the analytics team needs to inspect the dataset, verify its structure, and confirm that the data has been loaded correctly. 



Your task is to write a Python program that: 



Loads the stock market dataset. 
Displays the first 5 rows of the dataset. 
Displays the structure of the dataset, including column names, data types, and non-null values. 
This step ensures the dataset is suitable for further time series analysis and modeling.
